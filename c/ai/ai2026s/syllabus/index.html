Outline: (from Catalog)
• This course introduces the foundations of generative artificial intelligence. Understand the limits of artificial intelligence and explore the applications of generative artificial intelligence. The course content includes: variational autoencoders, generative adversarial networks, image generation technology, speech generation technology, text generation technology, prompt strategy to optimize model capabilities, tool combination to enhance model performance, and automatic detection of artificial intelligence generated content.
